Samsung F54 5G

Product Description

Samsung Galaxy F54 5G mobile was launched on 6th June 2023. The phone comes with:

  • 120 Hz refresh rate
  • 6.70-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 2400x1080 pixels (FHD+)

Specifications:

  • Processor: Octa-core Samsung Exynos 1380
  • RAM: 8GB
  • OS: Android 13 with OneUI 5
  • Battery: 6000mAh non-removable, supports proprietary fast charging

Cameras:

  1. Rear Camera Setup:
    • 108-megapixel primary camera
    • 8-megapixel camera
    • 2-megapixel camera
  2. Front Camera: 32-megapixel sensor

Storage:

  • 256GB of inbuilt storage
  • Expandable via microSD card (up to 1000GB)
